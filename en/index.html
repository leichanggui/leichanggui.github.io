I am Leichanggui, a bioinformatics researcher interested in post-GWAS analysis, single-cell multi-omics, public cohort mining, and disease mechanism discovery. This website organizes my research directions, project updates, analysis workflows, technical notes, and reusable resources.

This first version is designed as a publishable foundation. More formal affiliations, email, publication links, and project materials can be added progressively.

Focus Areas

Genetics

Post-GWAS Interpretation

Starting from GWAS signals, I integrate eQTLs, colocalization, fine-mapping, functional genomics, and literature evidence to prioritize disease-relevant genes and mechanisms.

Single Cell

Single-cell Multi-omics

I use scRNA-seq, cell type annotation, subclustering, trajectory analysis, and cell-cell communication analysis to understand disease microenvironments.

Disease Biology

Kidney Disease Mechanisms

For disease contexts such as diabetic kidney disease, I connect genetics, transcriptomics, and external validation evidence into interpretable mechanism stories.

Workflow

Reusable Pipelines

I build reusable workflows for data organization, statistical analysis, visualization, and report generation to improve reproducibility and delivery quality.

Highlights

  • Built a framework connecting post-GWAS signals to functional interpretation.
  • Performed single-cell clustering, annotation, subclustering, pseudotime, and cell communication analysis.
  • Integrated public GWAS, GEO, TCGA, DepMap, drug sensitivity, and structural druggability resources.
